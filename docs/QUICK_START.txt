PHOENIX FORGE ELITE: QUICK START GUIDE
===================================

STEP 1: INITIALIZATION
- Right-click PhoenixForge.exe and select "Run as Administrator" (Required for raw USB access).
- Ensure your RSA Cloud Vault is online (Check the top right of the dashboard).

STEP 2: CONNECTION
- Plug in the target device using a HIGH-SPEED, DATA-RATED USB-C cable. 
- Do not use cheap charging cables; Firehose and MTK VCOM protocols will drop packets and fail.
- Watch the "Pulse" sidebar. Wait for the silicon to be identified (e.g., Qualcomm 9008, Apple DFU).

STEP 3: AUTHORIZATION & SAFETY (MANDATORY)
- If the dashboard turns ORANGE (Qualcomm Gen 6), click "Request Cloud Signature" to spoof the BootROM.
- Once the dashboard is GREEN, you MUST click "Execute Pre-Flight Backup" (Shadow Rollback). 
- The system will NOT let you flash anything until EFS and NVRAM are safely backed up to your local /vault/ folder.

STEP 4: EXECUTION
- Select your target operation (e.g., Patch VBMeta, Extract Data, or Inject Poison Pill).
- Do not touch the device or the cable until the Chronicle Terminal outputs: "[+] SUCCESS: Device Rebooting."
